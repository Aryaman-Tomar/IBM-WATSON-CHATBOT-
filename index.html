Our Story

Founded in 2005, The Golden Spoon was born from a passion for authentic flavors and heartfelt hospitality. Our philosophy is simple: use the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients, honor traditional recipes with a modern twist, and create a warm, inviting atmosphere for every guest.

Our celebrated chef, Massimo Bottura, travels the world to bring back new inspirations, ensuring our menu is always exciting, innovative, and tells a story with every bite.
